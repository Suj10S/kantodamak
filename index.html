Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process a student visa for Japan?

Typically, the student visa process takes 2-3 months from application to approval. This includes language school admission, documentation preparation, and embassy processing.

What are the Japanese language proficiency levels required?

For most vocational schools, N4 level is sufficient. For university programs, N2 or higher is generally required. We offer courses from beginner N5 to advanced N1 levels.

Do you provide accommodation assistance for students going to Japan?
<ÊûÅp>Yes, we assist our students in finding suitable accommodation in Japan. Many of our partner language schools and universities also offer dormitory options.

Can I work part-time while studying in Japan?

Yes, international students with a valid student visa can work up to 28 hours per week during school sessions and up to 40 hours during holidays. We also provide guidance on finding part-time jobs in Japan.
