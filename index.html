About Kanto Japanese Language Centre - Premier Japanese Education in Nepal

Established with a vision to bridge cultures and create opportunities, Kanto Japanese Language Centre has been Nepal's premier destination for Japanese language education and Japan student visa assistance. Located in Damak, Jhapa, we specialize in JLPT preparation (N5 to N1), comprehensive visa processing, and university placement services for Nepali students aspiring to study in Japan.
